Our Mission

Guided by faith, driven by love, committed to our community

To Know Christ and Make Him Known

At Grace Community Church, we exist to glorify God by making disciples who love Jesus, serve others, and transform their world. We believe in creating a welcoming space where people from all walks of life can encounter the life-changing power of the Gospel.

Our vision is to be a church that reflects the diversity of our community, where every member is growing in their faith, using their gifts to serve, and actively sharing God's love with those around them.

We're committed to biblical teaching, authentic worship, genuine relationships, and compassionate outreach that meets both spiritual and physical needs.
