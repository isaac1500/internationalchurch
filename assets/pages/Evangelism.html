Evangelism Ministry

Reaching the lost with the life-changing message of Jesus Christ. We are committed to fulfilling the Great Commission through powerful outreach, discipleship, and equipping believers for evangelism.

Our Mission

The Evangelism Ministry of International Revival Ministries exists to train, equip, and mobilize believers to effectively share the Gospel of Jesus Christ in our communities, nation, and beyond.

We believe every Christian is called to be a witness (Acts 1:8) and that evangelism should be accompanied by demonstrations of God's power through healing, deliverance, and the working of miracles.

Our vision is to see multitudes come to salvation, churches planted, and communities transformed by the power of the Gospel.

Our Outreach Programs

Street Evangelism

Every Saturday, 9:00 AM

Teams go out to markets, bus parks, and public spaces to share the Gospel, pray for people, and demonstrate God's love in practical ways.

Campus Ministry

Weekly on University Campuses

Reaching students with the Gospel through Bible studies, fellowship meetings, and evangelistic events on campuses across the region.

Door-to-Door Evangelism

Monthly Neighborhood Outreach

Teams visit homes to share the Gospel, pray for families, and invite people to church services and special events.

Healing Crusades

Quarterly Events

Large-scale evangelistic events featuring powerful preaching, healing miracles, and mass conversions in communities across Uganda.

Evangelism Training

We equip believers with practical tools and biblical foundations for effective evangelism through:

  • School of Evangelism: 6-week intensive training program covering soul-winning techniques, apologetics, and power evangelism
  • Monthly Workshops: Practical sessions on street preaching, personal evangelism, and leading people to Christ
  • On-the-Job Training: New evangelists paired with experienced team members for mentoring
  • Power Evangelism Seminars: Teaching on operating in healing, deliverance, and miracles as part of evangelism
  • Digital Evangelism Training: How to effectively share the Gospel through social media and online platforms
