Welcome to International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church NAKAWA

A place of divine encounter where miracles happen, chains are broken, and lives are transformed by the power of God. Join us as we experience revival, healing, and deliverance through Christ Jesus.

Experience God's Healing Power

At International Revival Ministries, we believe in the present-day manifestation of God's healing and deliverance power. Join our weekly healing services and witness miracles as we pray in faith for all manner of sicknesses, diseases, and spiritual bondages.

Our Divine Mandate

International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church NAKAWA exists to proclaim the full Gospel of Jesus Christ with signs, wonders, and miracles following. We are committed to raising end-time revivalists who will demonstrate God's power through healing, deliverance, and prophetic ministry.

Our mission is threefold: to heal the sick, deliver the oppressed, and equip believers to walk in the supernatural power of God. We provide a spiritual hospital where the broken find restoration and the bound find freedom through the blood of Jesus.

Our Journey

International Revival Ministries was birthed from a divine vision to establish a center of revival where God's power would be manifested in healing and deliverance. Here are key milestones in our journey:

2005

Founded by Apostle John and Prophetess Sarah Muwanguzi after receiving a divine mandate to start a healing and deliverance ministry in Nakawa. The first service was held with 15 people in a rented space.

2010

Officially registered as International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church. The ministry gained recognition for remarkable healings and deliverances from demonic oppression.

2015

Launched the School of Healing and Deliverance to train ministers in the ministry of healing and spiritual warfare. Over 200 students enrolled in the first year.

2020

Acquired our current permanent home in Nakawa with a 500-seat sanctuary and dedicated prayer tower. The ministry expanded to include television broadcasts reaching millions.

Our Core Beliefs

These foundational truths guide our ministry and define our approach to healing and deliverance:

Divine Healing

We believe in healing as part of Christ's atonement (Isaiah 53:4-5, Matthew 8:16-17). God heals today through prayer, laying on of hands, and faith in His Word.

Deliverance

We practice deliverance ministry to set free those bound by demonic oppression (Mark 16:17, Luke 10:19). Every believer has authority over evil spirits.

Holy Spirit Power

We emphasize the baptism in the Holy Spirit with the evidence of speaking in tongues and the operation of spiritual gifts (Acts 1:8, 1 Corinthians 12:7-11).

Scriptural Authority

We base all ministry on the infallible Word of God. The Bible is our final authority for doctrine, practice, and Christian living (2 Timothy 3:16-17).

Prevailing Prayer

We maintain a strong prayer culture through our 24/7 prayer tower, believing prayer changes situations and releases God's power (James 5:16).

Global Revival

We are committed to taking this healing and deliverance message to all nations, training ministers to carry this revival fire worldwide (Mark 16:15).
