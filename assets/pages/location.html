Our Network of Revival Churches

International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church Nakawa oversees a growing network of revival centers across the region, each carrying the same fire for healing, deliverance, and transformation.

Ministry Network Overview

As part of our vision to spread revival fire, International Revival Ministries has planted and oversees multiple churches across different regions. Each church maintains its unique local expression while staying connected to our core values of healing, deliverance, and Holy Spirit empowerment.

Our network churches operate under the spiritual covering of International Revival Ministries Nakawa, with local pastors leading their congregations while participating in our apostolic training and fellowship programs.
