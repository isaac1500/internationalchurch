Our Community Mission

At International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church Nakawa, we believe in putting our faith into action through meaningful service to our community. Our Community Service Ministry is dedicated to addressing the physical, emotional, and spiritual needs of those around us.

Through various outreach programs and partnerships, we strive to be the hands and feet of Jesus in Nakawa, demonstrating God's love through practical acts of service and compassion.

Compassionate Service

Meeting needs with Christ-like love and dignity
