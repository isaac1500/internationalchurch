Hospitality Ministry

Creating a warm, welcoming environment where everyone feels at home in the house of God. We serve with love, ensuring every visitor experiences the love of Christ through our hospitality.

Welcome to God's Family

At International Revival Ministries, our Hospitality Ministry is dedicated to making every service, event, and gathering a welcoming experience for members and visitors alike. We believe that hospitality is more than just service—it's an expression of God's love.

Our team of dedicated volunteers works tirelessly to ensure that from the moment you step onto our premises, you feel valued, comfortable, and excited to worship with us.

Our Areas of Service

Greeters Team

The first smiling faces you'll see when you arrive! Our greeters welcome everyone at the doors, provide directions, and help first-time visitors feel comfortable.

Ushers Team

Our ushers assist with seating, distribute materials, maintain order during services, and are ready to help with any special needs during worship.

Welcome Center

The hub for newcomers! Here visitors can get information about the church, sign up for events, and receive a special welcome gift.

Events Hospitality

From conferences to special services, this team ensures all our events have excellent hospitality, including registration, seating, and refreshments.
