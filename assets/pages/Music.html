Music, Dance & Drama Ministry

Using creative arts to worship God, edify the church, and evangelize the world. We minister through melodies, movements, and messages that glorify Christ.

Ministering Through Arts

The Music, Dance & Drama Ministry at International Revival Ministries exists to usher people into God's presence through creative worship and to communicate the Gospel through performing arts.

We believe that as David danced before the Lord and the Psalms command us to praise Him with instruments and dancing, we are called to worship God with all our creative gifts. Our ministry teams are dedicated to excellence in both skill and spirit, offering our talents as worship to the King of kings.

Our Ministry Teams

Worship Team

Our anointed worship leaders and musicians facilitate encounters with God through passionate praise and intimate worship. The team includes vocalists, keyboardists, guitarists, drummers, and other instrumentalists.

Dance Ministry

Using creative movement and flags, our dance teams minister prophetically during worship services and evangelistic outreaches. We have groups for all ages - children, youth, and adults.

Drama Team

Through skits, plays, and dramatic presentations, this team communicates biblical truths in creative, engaging ways that touch hearts and transform lives.
