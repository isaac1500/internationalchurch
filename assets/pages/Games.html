About Our Sports Ministry

At International Revival Ministries Healing and Deliverance Church Nakawa, we believe in nurturing the whole person - spirit, soul, and body. Our Games & Sports Ministry provides a dynamic platform for physical activity, Christian fellowship, and outreach through sports.

We welcome participants of all skill levels, from beginners to experienced athletes, creating an environment where everyone can grow physically and spiritually while building meaningful relationships.

Physical Wellness

Promoting healthy lifestyles through regular exercise

Christian Fellowship

Building strong relationships through teamwork

Spiritual Growth

Integrating faith principles into sports activities
